Welcome to The Enu Project webpage

Our goal is to give incentives to society to not litter because the problem isnt the means of collecting trash, but putting it away. through many trials and errors we should focus on trash cans and their simplistic design. They do not give people a reason to throw away trash as it is easier to just dispose of their waste right next to them on the ground.
